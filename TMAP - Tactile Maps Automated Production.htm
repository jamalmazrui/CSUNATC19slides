What Is TMAP?

•      Tactile maps – neighborhood scale

•      Map files to be embossed & printed on paper

•      Automated, standardized

•      For wayfinding, independent travel, education, tactile literacy

(And What It’s Not)

•      Not fully customized for individuals

•      Not immediate, turn-by-turn directions

•      Not floor plans, transit maps, labeled campus maps

•      Not political, geographic, city, state, world map (yet)

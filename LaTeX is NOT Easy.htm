Research Gaps

u Few studies designed to enable visually impaired people as STEM content producers going beyond the consumer level.

u A lack of authoring tools available to independently produce scientific documents and materials.

Purpose of the Study

u To address the need of introducing an easy-to-write and accessible scientific document authoring tool for the blind:

• To define the scope of a scientific document.

• To highlight some issues of the conventional methods of the blind community for scientific document production.

• To detail the development research of Accessible RMarkdown Online Writer (AROW) as a practical solution.

• To demonstrate AROW system to prove its capability for highly accessible, inclusive, and extensible scientific document production.

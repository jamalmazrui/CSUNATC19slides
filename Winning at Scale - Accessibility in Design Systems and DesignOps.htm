2.  DESIGN OPS

The practice of DevOps provides efficiencies for developers with integration tools, automation, and strives for efficiency in the process of building and deploying software.

DesignOps looks to provide the same kind of efficiency and leverage for designers and design teams to allow them to design faster, better, and get products to market quicker.

Teams are leveraging Design Systems to do their work more efficiently, and they’re incorporating accessibility into those Design Systems.
